- Mission -

To provide a free platform for UMD Organizations to start a fundraiser.


- About Us -

At GoFundTerps, we provide an easy way for people to support their UMD Organization of choice. Taking inspiration from another popular donation website 'gofundme,' our goal is to provide a fundrasing platform specific to UMD. In the near future, we hope to expand our platform to other Universities & Colleges.
